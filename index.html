My Skills and Experiences

In my role as a Frontend Developer, my expertise includes:

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Redux, APIs, UX and Usability, Figma, Github, Dynamic web development, TypeScript, Node.js, MongoDB, and Agile methods (Scrum).


In addition to these core skills, I have experience working with Mantine and Tailwind CSS. Mantine is a modern React component library that provides a comprehensive set of components for building high-quality, responsive user interfaces. Tailwind CSS is a highly customizable, utility-first CSS framework for rapidly building custom designs and ensuring a consistent look and feel across a project.


I utilize my skills to create interactive and accessible websites while driving the development of larger projects. Whether working independently or collaborating within a team, my focus is on creating dynamic and engaging web experiences for users.

My Services

My experiences have led me through exciting roles in web development, particularly with Yoin Tech and Swedcon18. These two prominent tech companies prioritize innovative design, user experience, and technical expertise. While Yoin Tech focuses on scaling businesses by combining various areas of expertise, Swedcon18 aims to enhance business efficiency through tailored technology solutions and support.

Frontend Developer

At Yoin Tech, I worked as a Frontend Developer on a platform developed in TypeScript for business networking. The platform allowed users to search and collaborate with business contacts, and through its recommendation feature, they could expand within their network. The system was tailored for social sellers, emphasizing relationships, while also providing tools to update contacts and analyze network activity.

Frontend Developer

At Swedcon18, I worked as a Frontend Developer and had the privilege to be part of the team that developed a healthcare app, Carelyo Medtech. This app is a telehealth platform revolutionizing how people manage their health. It offers advanced digital services such as electronic medical records and lab services. I also gained experience working with both Next.js and Mantine.
